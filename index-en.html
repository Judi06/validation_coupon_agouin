FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions about Check-myPCS

How does Coupon Activation work?

Our system analyzes the structure of the top-up code and verifies its validity against our proprietary databases and algorithms. We compare the code format with those of partner operators and verify that it has not already been used or reported as fraudulent.

Is the service really free?

Yes, Basic Coupon Activation is completely free. We also offer premium options with advanced features like unlimited history or fraud alerts, but Simple Activation remains available to everyone at no cost.

Which operators are supported?

We currently support all major carriers (Orange, MTN, Vodafone, etc.) in several countries. Our list of compatible carriers is regularly expanding.

Is my personal data secure?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption for all transmitted data. The top-up codes you activate are not stored in our database after analysis.
